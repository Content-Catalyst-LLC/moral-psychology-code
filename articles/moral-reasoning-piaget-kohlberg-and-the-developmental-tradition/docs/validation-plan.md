# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic cases are not interpreted as real children, adolescents, students, families, schools, communities, cultures, organizations, institutions, or moral worth.
6. Stage-like categories are not interpreted as real Kohlberg stage assessments.
7. Justice, care, conventional sensitivity, and autonomy outputs are not interpreted as real psychological scores.
8. Stage-domain mismatch cases are not interpreted as real-person classifications.
9. Outputs are not used for student assessment, school evaluation, hiring, promotion, discipline, workplace surveillance, psychological assessment, legal decisions, or automated moral judgment.
10. Article-level outputs remain clearly labeled as synthetic demonstration data.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- longitudinal moral development datasets
- moral judgment interview coding
- social domain theory tasks
- moral-conventional distinction measures
- child and adolescent fairness reasoning studies
- care reasoning and relational moral development data
- moral education intervention data
- school climate and discipline datasets
- cultural psychology and moral development datasets
- reasoning-action gap studies

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
