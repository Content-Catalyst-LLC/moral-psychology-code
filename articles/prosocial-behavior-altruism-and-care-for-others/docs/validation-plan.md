# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic cases are not interpreted as real persons, caregivers, patients, families, workers, students, institutions, cultures, communities, or care systems.
6. Empathic concern is not interpreted as a real empathy assessment.
7. Altruistic motive is not interpreted as a real motive or virtue score.
8. Burnout is not interpreted as a diagnosis or clinical measure.
9. Prosocial probability is not interpreted as a prediction of real conduct.
10. Outputs are not used for empathy assessment, altruism scoring, caregiver assessment, employee screening, student assessment, healthcare triage, labor allocation, legal decisions, or automated moral judgment.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- empathy and empathic concern measures
- prosocial behavior observations
- donation, volunteering, and helping datasets
- cooperation and public-goods experiments
- altruistic-motive and mixed-motive studies
- caregiver burden and burnout measures
- relational closeness and responsibility scales
- institutional support and voice-safety data
- developmental prosocial learning datasets
- cross-cultural prosocial behavior research
- organizational care and cooperation studies
- policy datasets on care labor, family leave, public support, and community infrastructure

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
