# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic cases are not interpreted as real adults, older adults, families, caregivers, institutions, cultures, or communities.
6. Adult moral-development profile is not interpreted as a real moral-development score.
7. Moral action probability is not interpreted as a prediction of real conduct.
8. Caregiving burden is not interpreted as a caregiver assessment.
9. Decision capacity is not interpreted as a cognitive test.
10. Outputs are not used for elder assessment, caregiver assessment, workplace assessment, healthcare triage, psychological assessment, legal decisions, policy allocation, or automated moral judgment.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- adult moral identity scales
- prosociality and altruism measures across adulthood
- moral emotion measures across later life
- caregiving burden and support datasets
- aging cognition and moral decision-making tasks
- generativity and legacy concern scales
- retirement and role-transition studies
- elder-care institutional ethics research
- longitudinal adult moral development data
- life-review, regret, repair, and wisdom studies
- organizational voice safety and adult moral action studies
- cross-cultural adult moral development research

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
