# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic cases are not interpreted as real people, workers, students, organizations, cultures, or communities.
6. Broad personality is not interpreted as a real personality assessment.
7. Character strength is not interpreted as a virtue or moral worth score.
8. Moral action probability is not interpreted as a prediction of real conduct.
9. Code comments and docs clearly identify the data as synthetic.
10. Outputs are not used for hiring, employee screening, student evaluation, workplace surveillance, psychological assessment, disciplinary decisions, legal decisions, political targeting, or automated moral judgment.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- Big Five personality measures
- moral identity scales
- empathy and compassion measures
- character-strength instruments
- moral action and behavioral-task data
- self-interest bias experiments
- organizational voice safety surveys
- moral courage and whistleblowing studies
- person-situation longitudinal designs
- CAPS-style if-then behavioral signatures
- cultural and political moral variation datasets
- institutional accountability and ethical climate research

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
