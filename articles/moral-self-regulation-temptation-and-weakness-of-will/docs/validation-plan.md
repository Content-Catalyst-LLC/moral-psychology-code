# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic cases are not interpreted as real persons, students, employees, leaders, organizations, institutions, cultures, political groups, or communities.
6. Moral judgment is not interpreted as a real moral reasoning assessment.
7. Self-regulatory capacity is not interpreted as a real psychological assessment.
8. Weakness of will is not interpreted as a clinical, legal, workplace, or educational measure.
9. Repair capacity is not interpreted as a psychological, clinical, legal, or organizational assessment.
10. Outputs are not used for employee screening, hiring, promotion, discipline, student assessment, workplace surveillance, psychological assessment, legal decisions, or automated moral judgment.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- self-control and self-regulation measures
- implementation-intention intervention data
- moral judgment and behavior studies
- temptation and delay-of-gratification tasks
- fatigue, workload, and ethical decision-making datasets
- rationalization and self-deception studies
- moral identity and action data
- organizational ethical-climate measures
- role pressure and institutional incentive research
- repair, apology, and accountability behavior studies
- digital impulse and outrage behavior datasets

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
