# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Intuition, reflection, norm sensitivity, and consequence sensitivity remain distinct.
6. Blame is modeled separately from moral judgment.
7. Power condition and group identity salience are documented as contextual variables.
8. Synthetic variables are not interpreted as real measurements.
9. No output is described as a moral score for real persons.
10. Model assumptions and limitations are documented.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- vignette-based moral judgment experiments
- moral dilemma response datasets
- process-dissociation or CNI-style model outputs
- blame and wrongness judgment datasets
- response-time or process-tracing data
- cross-cultural moral judgment samples
- political ideology and moral judgment data
- power and authority manipulation studies

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, ethical review where appropriate, privacy safeguards, preregistration where possible, and careful construct validation.
