# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and methodological transparency.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Experimental conditions are documented.
6. Synthetic variables are not interpreted as real measurements.
7. Wrongness, blame, action tendency, and latent constructs remain conceptually distinct.
8. Social desirability and measurement error are acknowledged.
9. Cross-cultural and developmental limitations are documented.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- moral-vignette datasets
- developmental interview data
- norm-learning tasks
- cross-cultural moral judgment studies
- blame and punishment judgment datasets
- moral identity scales
- behavioral helping or punishment tasks
- longitudinal moral development data

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, and careful construct validation.
