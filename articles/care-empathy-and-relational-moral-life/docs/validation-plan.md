# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic cases are not interpreted as real caregivers, patients, families, workers, students, organizations, schools, workplaces, cultures, or communities.
6. Empathy is not interpreted as a real empathy score.
7. Burnout is not interpreted as a diagnosis or clinical measure.
8. Caring response probability is not interpreted as a prediction of real conduct.
9. High-empathy low-care cases are not interpreted as real-person risk classifications.
10. Outputs are not used for caregiver assessment, patient assessment, employee screening, school assessment, healthcare triage, burnout diagnosis, labor allocation, legal decisions, or automated moral judgment.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- empathy and perspective-taking measures
- caregiver burden and burnout scales
- relational care and support datasets
- healthcare staffing and care-quality datasets
- school climate and student-support datasets
- elder care and disability support research
- organizational voice-safety and ethical-climate measures
- qualitative interviews about care, dependence, and repair
- care-network and mutual-aid data
- longitudinal caregiver support and sustainability studies
- policy datasets on care labor, paid leave, staffing, and public support

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
