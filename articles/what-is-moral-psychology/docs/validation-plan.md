# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic people and organizations are not treated as real persons or institutions.
6. Judgment-action gap outputs are not treated as moral failure diagnoses.
7. Moral identity, empathy, and self-regulation values are not treated as real assessments.
8. Institutional pressure and accountability values are not treated as real audits.
9. Outputs are not used for employee screening, student assessment, workplace surveillance, hiring, promotion, discipline, legal decisions, or automated moral judgment.
10. Article-level outputs remain clearly labeled as synthetic demonstration data.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- validated moral identity scales
- validated moral disengagement scales
- moral conviction measures
- institutional climate surveys
- longitudinal studies of moral action
- behavioral measures of prosocial conduct
- organizational reporting and retaliation data
- qualitative work on moral environments
- mixed-method studies of repair infrastructure

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
