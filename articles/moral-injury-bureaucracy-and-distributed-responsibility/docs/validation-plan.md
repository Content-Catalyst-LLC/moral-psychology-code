# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic workers are not interpreted as real workers.
6. Moral injury risk is not interpreted as a clinical or occupational assessment.
7. Institutional betrayal and distributed responsibility are documented as contextual variables.
8. Accountability clarity, protected voice, and repair capacity are documented as structural dampeners.
9. Code comments and docs clearly identify the data as synthetic.
10. Outputs are not used for screening, surveillance, diagnosis, discipline, targeting, or workplace decision-making.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- de-identified moral injury survey data
- occupational moral distress datasets
- healthcare staffing and resource-constraint indicators
- institutional betrayal measures
- organizational climate and voice-protection measures
- incident reporting and escalation pathway data
- qualitative interview coding schemes
- responsibility-mapping documentation
- longitudinal worker wellbeing data

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
