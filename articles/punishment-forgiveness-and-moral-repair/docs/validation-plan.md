# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic cases are not interpreted as real legal, moral, institutional, or interpersonal cases.
6. Punishment score is not interpreted as a sentencing recommendation.
7. Forgiveness probability is not interpreted as a victim expectation, moral demand, or therapeutic recommendation.
8. Moral repair is not interpreted as a real measure of healing or justice.
9. Code comments and docs clearly identify the data as synthetic.
10. Outputs are not used for sentencing, discipline, legal advice, victim assessment, offender assessment, or restorative-justice decision-making.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- restorative justice process evaluations
- victim recognition and safety measures
- apology and restitution studies
- offender accountability and remorse measures
- institutional trust surveys
- legal sanction and public norm-reaffirmation data
- truth and reconciliation process datasets
- transitional justice case studies
- organizational misconduct repair studies
- public apology and reparations datasets
- longitudinal repair and trust rebuilding research

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
