# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic groups are not interpreted as real groups.
6. Moral exclusion risk is not interpreted as a real-person or real-group score.
7. Polarization distance is documented as a simplified analytic indicator.
8. Institutional trust and cross-group contact are documented as dampening variables.
9. Code comments and docs clearly identify the data as synthetic.
10. Outputs are not used for profiling, targeting, surveillance, persuasion, classification, or decision-making.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- social identity and group identification surveys
- affective polarization measures
- institutional trust measures
- media exposure and selective exposure datasets
- cross-group contact and deliberation studies
- dehumanization and moral exclusion scales
- partisan bias experiments
- longitudinal public-opinion data
- qualitative group-narrative coding
- institutional legitimacy and procedural-trust datasets

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
