# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic groups are not interpreted as real populations.
6. Moral exclusion risk is not interpreted as an assessment tool.
7. Propaganda exposure is not treated as identical to persuasion.
8. Democratic trust and cross-group contact are documented as dampening variables.
9. Code comments and docs clearly identify the data as synthetic.
10. Outputs are not used for targeting, profiling, surveillance, or persuasion.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- media exposure datasets
- survey data on institutional trust
- affective polarization measures
- social media outrage datasets
- political misinformation or propaganda exposure studies
- dehumanization and moral exclusion scales
- democratic trust and cross-group contact measures
- longitudinal public-opinion data

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, ethical review where appropriate, privacy safeguards, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
