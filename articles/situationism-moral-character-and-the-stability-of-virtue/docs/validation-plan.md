# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic cases are not interpreted as real persons, employees, students, organizations, schools, workplaces, cultures, or communities.
6. Character strength is not interpreted as a real virtue score.
7. Moral action probability is not interpreted as a prediction of real conduct.
8. High-character low-action cases are not interpreted as real-person risk classifications.
9. Voice safety is not interpreted as a workplace, school, or institutional assessment.
10. Outputs are not used for employee screening, hiring, promotion, discipline, student assessment, workplace surveillance, psychological assessment, legal decisions, or automated moral judgment.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- repeated-measures moral behavior data
- person-situation experiments
- CAPS-style if-then response signatures
- Big Five personality measures
- VIA character strengths measures
- moral identity scales
- self-regulation and depletion measures
- organizational voice-safety surveys
- accountability and ethical-climate studies
- bystander and authority-pressure experiments
- moral courage and whistleblowing data
- institutional incentive and role-pressure datasets
- longitudinal character-development research

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
