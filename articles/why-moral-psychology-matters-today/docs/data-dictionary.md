# Data Dictionary

## Synthetic variables

| Variable | Description |
|---|---|
| `political_polarization` | Synthetic standardized indicator of polarized moralized public conflict |
| `organizational_risk` | Synthetic standardized indicator of institutional moral risk, ethical fading, and distributed responsibility |
| `developmental_moral_learning` | Synthetic standardized indicator of moral learning and developmental relevance |
| `cross_cultural_variation` | Synthetic standardized indicator of moral variation across cultures and politics |
| `technology_amplification` | Synthetic standardized indicator of digital moral amplification, outrage, and platform-mediated visibility |
| `moral_injury_burden` | Synthetic standardized indicator of moral injury, institutional betrayal, and conscience strain |
| `moral_psychology_significance` | Synthetic combined score representing the modeled social significance of moral psychology |
| `significance_band` | Quartile grouping of the synthetic significance score |
