# Validation Plan

This scaffold is synthetic and conceptual. Validation focuses on reproducibility, interpretive clarity, and responsible use.

## Checks

1. Scripts run from the article root folder.
2. Output folders are created automatically.
3. Generated tables are written to `outputs/tables`.
4. Figure outputs are written to `outputs/figures` where applicable.
5. Synthetic cases are not interpreted as real legal, institutional, or interpersonal cases.
6. Responsibility score is not interpreted as a real-person responsibility assessment.
7. Blame probability is not interpreted as a blame recommendation.
8. Accountability score is not interpreted as a legal, institutional, employment, or disciplinary decision tool.
9. Accountability quality is not interpreted as an audit, certification, or justice measure.
10. Outputs are not used for employee discipline, legal advice, offender assessment, institutional audits, psychological assessment, or automated moral judgment.

## Possible empirical extensions

Future empirical work could include:

- moral responsibility judgment experiments
- blame attribution studies
- epistemic condition and willful ignorance studies
- accountability and institutional trust surveys
- organizational failure case studies
- professional ethics and role-duty data
- incident reporting and oversight datasets
- public-sector accountability frameworks
- victim recognition and repair measures
- institutional reform and recurrence-prevention studies

Any empirical extension should include source documentation, privacy safeguards, ethical review where appropriate, measurement validation, and careful interpretation.
